Install Beta

After installing

  1. Tap Install when prompted, then wait for the app to appear on your home screen.
  2. Open Settings → General → VPN & Device Management.
  3. Tap the developer certificate and tap Trust.
  4. Open the app — you're all set.

This beta is only available for registered test devices. If installation fails, your device UDID may not be included in the provisioning profile.
