Scanning Recipes

How do I scan a recipe?

  1. Tap the + button on the main screen
  2. Choose Scan Recipe to photograph a cookbook page
  3. Position the page in the camera viewfinder and take the photo
  4. AI will automatically extract the recipe title, ingredients, and instructions
  5. Review the extracted recipe, make any edits, and save

What kind of recipes can I scan?

Cookbook Curator PRO works with printed cookbook pages, recipe cards, and magazine clippings. The AI works best with clearly printed text. Handwritten recipes may have lower accuracy.

When should I use AI Scan vs Step By Step?

AI Scan is great for recipes that fit on a single page — just snap one photo and the AI extracts everything automatically.

For recipes that span multiple pages, Step By Step is the better choice. It lets you photograph each page separately and combines them into one complete recipe.

The scan didn't extract correctly. What should I do?

Ensure the page is well-lit and the text is in focus. You can always edit the extracted recipe after scanning — tap any field to make corrections.

Organizing Recipes

How do I organize recipes into cookbooks?

When you scan or add a recipe, you can assign it to a cookbook. You can also create new cookbooks from the main screen. Each recipe belongs to one cookbook and can include a page number for easy reference back to the original book.

Can I search my recipes?

Yes! Use the search bar at the top of the recipe list to search by recipe title, ingredients, or cookbook name. You can also filter recipes by tags and favorites.

How do I tag recipes?

Open any recipe and tap Edit. You can add tags like "Dinner," "Quick," "Vegetarian," or any custom tag. Use tags to filter and find recipes quickly.

Sharing & Exporting

How do I share a recipe?

Open the recipe and tap the share button in the top right. You can share as text, a recipe card image, or a PDF. The share sheet lets you send via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or any other app.

How do I export all my recipes?

Go to Settings → Export All Recipes. You can export your entire collection as a JSON file (for backup) or a PDF (for printing). The PDF includes a title page and all recipes formatted for US Letter paper.

iCloud Sync

Do my recipes sync across devices?

Yes, if iCloud is enabled. Your recipes sync automatically via iCloud across all your Apple devices signed in with the same Apple ID. Make sure iCloud is enabled in Settings → iCloud within the app.

iCloud sync isn't working. What should I check?

  • Ensure you're signed into iCloud on your device (iOS Settings → Apple ID → iCloud)
  • Check that iCloud sync is enabled within the app (Settings → iCloud toggle)
  • Make sure you have available iCloud storage
  • Try closing and reopening the app — sync can take a moment on first launch
  • Ensure you're connected to the internet

Subscriptions & Free Scans

How many free scans do I get?

Free users get 5 AI scans per month, which reset on the 1st of each month. You can also earn up to 4 bonus scans by leaving a review on the App Store (+2) or sharing the app with a friend (+2). Manual recipe entry is always unlimited.

What does a Pro subscription include?

Pro gives you 200 AI scans per month, full iCloud sync across all your Apple devices, and the ability to export and share your recipes. Pro is available as a monthly or annual subscription — the annual plan saves you 30%.

What happens when I run out of free scans?

When your free scans are used up, you'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro. You can also add recipes manually at any time, or wait until the 1st of the next month when your free scans reset.

Account & Privacy

What data does the app collect?

Your recipes are stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud container. Photos sent for AI scanning are processed in real time and not retained. See our full Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual recipes or cookbooks by swiping left. To delete everything, go to Settings → Clear All Data. To delete your account entirely, contact us at the email below.
